Introduction

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs various tasks. It can store, retrieve, and process information to help with different tasks.

History

Computers have evolved from early mechanical devices to modern-day digital machines. The development of computers began in the 19th century with pioneers like Charles Babbage.
